When a feed URL's connection is dropped mid-response, the unhandled exception crashes the entire script instead of recording the error and continuing with remaining feeds.

**Fix needed:** Add `RemoteDisconnected` (and optionally `ConnectionResetError`, `BrokenPipeError`) to the except clause on line 251 of `news/scripts/collect_candidates.py`.

**Affected feed:** The error occurred during `fetch(feed['url'])` in the main loop. Without additional context in the traceback, the specific failing URL cannot be identified from the crash alone. Once the fix is applied, the script will record `fetch_errors` per-feed and continue collecting from remaining sources.
